Welcome

Problem:Due to covid many people have not been able
to interact with their friends and family. Research shows
that healthy relationships and human interactions can help a
person's mental health, but unfortunatle covid has affected many
people's relationships with others. We wanted to build a solution
that would keep people connected. Often times, text messages and
emails can get lost in someone's inbox, and most phone calls can be meaningless.

Solution:We thought, that a great way for people to stay connected
with each other was through meaningful letters. Introducing lettr,
